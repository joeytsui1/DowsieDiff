Dowsie Diff

I am Joey Tsui, I enjoy playing Team Fight Tactics which led me to make this project!

Dowsie Diff is a data visualization of stats from players who played TFT by Riot Games. This interface allows players to easily view their last 20 Ranked Games match history, including information such as win rate, average placement, and most frequently used champions. This app will automatically render data from my account upon opening, to begin enter your summoner's name!
